FAQ
Which folder?
Choose the shared folder with the saved schedules. In most setups that is app/data.
Teams or SharePoint?
Sync the shared files to your computer first, then open DaySchedule from that synced folder.
Can multiple people edit?
Safest answer: one editor at a time.
Audience vs Specific People?
Audience is the group. Specific People is for named people.
Where do I change the look?
Use Customize.
Missing someone else’s changes?
Return to the library and reopen the shared schedule.
When should I export a file?
Only for backup or when the browser cannot auto-save.
